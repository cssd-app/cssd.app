For orthopedic instruments, the standard procedure is:

1. Pre-clean with enzymatic detergent
2. Ultrasonic cleaning for 10 minutes
3. Rinse thoroughly with distilled water
4. Inspect for residual soil and damage
5. Package in appropriate wrap or container
6. Steam sterilize at 132°C for 4 minutes
